# Akadia PlantUML Business Process Summary

Generated package contains 29 PlantUML files:

- BP-00: End-to-end overview
- BP-01 to BP-28: Detailed business process diagrams

The diagrams are activity diagrams with swimlanes and are designed to be opened directly by the PlantUML VS Code extension.

## Main Groups

1. Platform and identity: BP-01 to BP-04
2. Academic setup: BP-05 to BP-07
3. Payment configuration: BP-08 to BP-14
4. Billing: BP-15 to BP-20
5. Checkout and payment: BP-21 to BP-26
6. Settlement, ledger and reporting: BP-27 to BP-28

## Important Technical Notes

- Diagrams avoid PlantUML element references such as `note right of Platform` to prevent swimlane-related syntax errors.
- Titles use ASCII hyphen instead of em dash.
- Current MVP implemented routes are reflected in notes inside the related diagrams.
- Future processes from the source DOCX are still included and marked as future scope in notes.
